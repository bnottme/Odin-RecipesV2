Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken

By Divinesolace21 Updated on March 27, 2024


This crockpot Buffalo chicken is great for hearty sandwiches that will please those who
love Buffalo chicken wings. This recipe is perfect for those days spent watching football. I
like to top these with blue cheese or ranch dressing.


Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halve
  • 1 (17.5 fluid ounce) bottle Buffalo wing sauce, divided
  • Â½ (1 ounce) package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.


  2. Place chicken breasts into the slow cooker; pour in 3/4 of the wing sauce and ranch dressing mix.
    Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours.


  3. Shred chicken in the cooker with two forks. Stir in butter.


  4. Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls. Serve with remaining Buffalo sauce.


  5. Serve and enjoy!
